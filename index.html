COOLITON

A wristband that keeps you cool

Product

CoolitOn is a wristband that passively cools down the human body without the need for external power.

Use

CoolitOn is an elegant easy to use wristband able to provide cooling without the need of external power sources.

This is achievable by utilizing phase change materials (PCM) in a customized way.

What we do

Innovative products
We take pride in what we do.

Problem

The body’s heating mechanism in some people is overactive to a point where the individual may sweat more than five times the normal. When sweating is at such levels it can be embarrassing, and anxiety-inducing.

Solution

Heat is absorbed in the wristband allowing the manipulation of cooling to the body through absorbing the heat from the pulse points with an operating time of up to 5 hours before a recharge is required. This will minimize heat induced health problems

Market

Our market includes hyperhidrosis patients who amount to 5% of the world's population, the 75% of women going through menopause that experience hot flushes, athletes and other people exposed to high temperatures. Our main market is EU, USA and Canada

Finance

We took the time to understand and uncover the cost and pain, we know how much it will mean to you, the payoff will be in every sense of the word – financially, physically, intellectually, emotionally, from moving to the solution that we have for you. The price of the product will be offered soon.

Plan

We have a detailed go to market plan and preparing to sign the needed agreements with manufacturers in China and US.
